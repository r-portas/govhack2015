How's My Suburb?

Welcome to How's My Suburb? This is a service for finding information regarding community services and infrastructure. Enter your desired suburb or postcode to find out what services are available in your area as well as other information regarding available public transport, internet services and much more.

Hospitals

Finds the closest hospitals to a given suburb and display the number of beds and facilities they have

Public transport

Displays all public transport stops on a map for a given suburb, allowing the user to see the closest stop to them

Broadband Availability

Finds information on the internet connections within a suburb including fibre and mobile availability

Average Suburb Tax Rates

Shows average tax rates from suburbs, including medicare levels and HELP debt information

Crime Data

Finds information relating to crime in each suburb and compares it to the average crime rate
